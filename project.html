About Communote

Communote offers a web-based open source communication platform for enterprises, institutions, NGOs and communities. The Communote project was initiated by Communardo, a leading social business and collaboration solution provider.

Contribute

Communote is an open source project. Your participation in the further development of the software will be highly welcomed by the user community. Feel free to let the team know your feature suggestions and bug reports.

You may also use the repository to access the source code and documentation to contribute own code.
